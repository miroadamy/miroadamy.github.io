From today's Steve Jobs' keynote at Macworld 2007:

"I skate to where the puck is going to be, not to where it has been. "
Wayne Gretzky

I guess everybody is going to write about iPhone - which is OK because it is indeed very interesting gadget and it stole the show. To bad it will be 6 more months until it will be available in the US and (my best hopes) 8-9 months to have it here in Canada.

Three other things what I secretly hoped that will be announced:

1) a date when Leopard will be out (I kind of hoped it will be out when Vista hits the streets) and more details on features
2) announcement that Mac Pro will be upgraded to dual-quadcore CPU. Would not it be great to have machine with 8 cores, 8 GB RAM and 2 TB diskspace ?
3) ultra-light 10-11" notebook with speech and writing recognition

I guess we'll have to wait ...

But it was better than a movie. Great show, Mr. Jobs.
